Blackjack Rules!!! :D

Overview!

Blackjack is a fairly simple game. You and the dealer each draw two cards. You get to see the dealer's first card, but their second card is hidden. The goal is to draw cards with a total value as close to 21 as possible without going over. If you go over, then you bust and lose. When it's your turn you will see your total value from the two cards you have drawn, and then you will choose to either draw another card to add to your value or pass and let the dealer go. Once you pass or draw to 21, then the dealer goes. The dealer's second card will be revealed and the dealer will continue drawing until they either end up with a higher value than you or until they bust. The card values are as follows: 2-10 are face value, jack, queen, and king are ten, and an ace is 11.

Points!

Points are super simple here. You earn triple the amount of points you put in. However, if you lose then you lose double your bet. If you and the dealer both draw with 21 then, your points are returned.
